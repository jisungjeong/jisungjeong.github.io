MANAGEMENT TEAM

OUR MANAGEMENT


SOONSU, an eco-friendly cosmetics company started to run its business with four co-founder since April, 2019.
SOONSU is consist of FIVE major divisons: marketing and sale, R&D, customer service, sustainability and SOONSU China.
Each division cooperate with open-minded and tries to improve quality of product and cutomer's satisfaction.
They are always keeping SOONSU's vision and principles in mind.

ORGANIZATION STRUCTURE


DETAILS


MARKETING and SALE

Director of Marketing and sales is SUNGJOONG KIM. The sales and marketing division sets individual sales rep quotas, as well as the overall volume goal for the company. To achieve sales goals, it creates bonus and commissions structures. The division uses past sales figures and expert projections to estimate which products will sell where and in what amounts.

R&D

Director of R&D is Haein Kim. Soonsu's R&D division is in charge of how to make our products with keeping our 5 principles. Water is commonly used as the first (majority) ingredient in most skin, hair, and body products. Instead of water, Soonsu uses beneficial organic floral hydrosols, organic aloe juice, organic fermented rice water (sake) and organic teas. It's also common to use thickeners to expand / add volume to economize formulas but Soonsu formulas are never diluted with cheap fillers. Every ingredient in each Soonsu formula is included for its benefit.

SUSTAINABILITY

Director of sustainability division is Jisung Jung. With respond to growing concern of enviornment, Soonsu are trying hard to acheive SDGs from UN. We are committed to minimizing our carbon footprint in all areas of our business. This is illustrated in how we develop our products (biodegradable formulas) how we package (recycled/ recyclable packaging, biodegradable packing material from corn starch), how we print (non toxic and soy inks), and even how we landscape (drought resistant plants watered from the condensation collected from our roof). Our 8 acre headquarter property is 100% solar powered, and we extend this emphasis on sustainability to every aspect of Soonsu.

SOONSU CHINA

Director of Soonsu China is Hyeyun Son. Considerable sales of Soonsu are come from China. It become important to fulfill needs of Chinese customers. We manage various legal problem in this division as well. We cooperate with other division to develop new, trendy products for Chinese customer.
