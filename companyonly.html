ABOUT US

COMPANY INFORMATION

In Korean, SOONSU means the state of not being mixed with anything else.

Innovations, Technology

Natural fragrances are usually from essential oils, which are limited to
certain herbs, citrus, and flowers. To avoid limitation, we adapted the same technology
that food manufacturers use to create "natural flavors" to isolate scents
and create a more broad range of "natural fragrances".

OUR VISION


Our company, SOONSU, only deals in cosmetics composed of 100% pure ingredient. SOONSU puts a lot of effort to provide eco-friendly, pure and healthy products. We also try to educate our values that careful selecting of ingredient is important.SOONSU strives to live with empathy and consideration for being environmentally sustainable and enhance quality of lives of both human and animals. Our goal 2019 is to maximize common good through SOONSU’s product.

OUR PRODUCT



Enjoy our product!




Cruelty-free and vegan cosmetics are our main items. Benefits of our products come from not only the fact that they consist of natural ingredients but also our unique health formula of our R&D. We also sell skin care and hair and body product with same ingredients. All products are pigmented using domestic fruits and vegetables. SOONSU never use artificial colors. Only plant dyed product is sold on our website. Same with color, SOONSU never use artificial flavors. We use natural fragrances from limited certain herbs, citrus, and flowers.

Sustainability

We are committed to minimizing our carbon footprint in all areas of our business.
This is illustrated in how we develop our products in biodegradable formulas
how we use recyclable packaging,
how we print, and even how we landscape .
Our headquarter property is 100% solar powered,
and we extend this emphasis on sustainability to every aspect of SOONSU.

ONLY use LED light
